ABOUT US

Turn yarn into magic

We feel very blessed to wake up and go to work where we are surrounded by beautiful crochets everyday. We want to share our love and passion of crochets with you!

Our goal at Eng's Crochet is to produce beautiful crochet that you will never forget. Because we're not just passionate about crochets we are passionate about our amazing customers. Every arrangement that we make is unique; decors, gifts, sympathy, just because, and everywhere in-between.

Although crochets will always be our first love... We have much more inside our shop! Great unique gifts and clothes for everyone in your life .. and we are constantly making new items in! So stop by and see us! We hope you enjoy our work as much as we enjoy bringing it to you.
